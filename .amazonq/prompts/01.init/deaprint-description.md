# Service Description
DeaPrint is a photography service. It is a one-person company run by Kasia.

Domain: deaprint.pl

The main activity is taking photos for documents.

Apart from document photos, the service offers:

- photo prints in photo labs and on ink printer, including high-quality prints up to A3
- enhancing photo quality using Photoshop
- printing documents
- taking photos for portfolios, social media, family photos, etc.
- design and production of photo calendars and photobooks, including photobooks for schools

The services are described in deaprint-services-prices.md

The company has many good reviews on Google, mainly for the excellent quality of photos and awesome customer service.
