## Content Tone and Style

### Voice
- **Professional but warm**: Expert but approachable
- **Patient and understanding**: Especially regarding children
- **Clear and direct**: No jargon, easy to understand
- **Multilingual friendly**: Simple language that translates well

### Writing Style
- **Short paragraphs**: Easy to scan
- **Bullet points**: For lists and features
- **Active voice**: "We take photos" not "Photos are taken"
- **Customer-focused**: Benefits over features

### Call-to-Actions
- "Book Your Appointment"
- "Contact Us Today"
- "View Our Portfolio"
- "Get a Quote"
- "Call Now: 882947647"
- "Message on WhatsApp"
